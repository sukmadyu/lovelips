About Us

PT. Coding Indonesia berdiri tahun 2013 menyusul kegembiraan anak-anak belajar coding pada program liburan.

Pada 2015, PT. Coding Indonesia mengelola tiga lokasi: Cipete, Kemang dan Bintaro, serta ekstrakurikuler di sekolah-sekolah swasta di DKI Jakarta. Lokasi berikutnya adalah Kelapa Gading dan Depok.

Who We Are

Wahyudi dan Kurie Suditomo, co-founders PT. Coding Indonesia, foto artikel www.poster.co.id

WAHYUDI

Wahyudi is a veteran in software development industry. He still codes his own applications on daily basis. Wahyudi learned to code in Berlin, where he lived for 10 years. Working for corporate and startups in Berlin and Munich gave him some valuable experiences from both side of the culture. Moved to east coast USA to pursue his career as software developer, he worked in New York & New Jersey for 6 years, before returning home in Jakarta to build his own companies.

Follow @Wahyudi

KURIE SUDITOMO

Kurie Suditomo spent 10 years in journalism, writing and editing for national politics and international news, as well as giving an ample contribution to the in-depth and feature stories in the arts and cultural section of Tempo Magazine. In 2005, she graduated from Ohio University, Athens with a master's degree in Southeast Asian Studies supported by Fulbright Program. Previously working as a representative for United States-Indonesia communities, Kurie's interests span as vast as national and international politics, international youth exchanges, museum and cultural heritage in the archipelago and digital media.

Follow @NengKurie
